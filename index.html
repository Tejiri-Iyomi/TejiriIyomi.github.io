    Data Cleaning in SQL

    To prepare the World Layoffs dataset for analysis, I performed a thorough data cleaning process using SQL. This involved removing records with missing or incomplete key information and standardizing text fields such as company names, industries, and countries to ensure consistency throughout the dataset. I also identified and eliminated duplicate entries to maintain data integrity. Additionally, I validated date and numeric fields to remove any incorrect or illogical values, such as future dates or negative layoff counts. Finally, I resolved inconsistencies in naming conventions to improve accuracy in grouping and analysis. These steps collectively ensured the dataset was clean, reliable, and ready for insightful analysis and visualization.
